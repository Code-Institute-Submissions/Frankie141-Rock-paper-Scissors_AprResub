Instructions

Aim of the game:

  1. Select either "Rock, Paper or Scissors".
  2. Paper beats rock, rock beats scissors and scissors beats paper.
  3. Paper beats rock, rock beats scissors and scissors beats paper.
  4. Depending on the choices made by the player or computer the winner will score a point.
  5. There will be 5 rounds in total.
  1. Step 1: Make your choice, "Rock, paper or scissors"
  2. Step 2 The computer makes a choice.
  3. Step 3 The score board is updated after each choice
  4. Step 4 After 5 rounds the game ends and the winner is announced
